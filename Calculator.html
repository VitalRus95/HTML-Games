      About

      You have a grid of digits and a status bar below it showing your current score, goal, the number of steps taken, and the queue of math operations. Your task is to reach the score goal using basic arithmetic operations (+, -, *, /). Clicking a digit on the grid will perform the leftmost operation in the queue (for example, /), using your current score (say, 12) as the first variable and the selected digit (e.g., 4) as the second one. In our example, the result then will be: 12 / 4 = 3. Good luck!
